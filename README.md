# 📊 Business Insights Report (EDA) – Bank Marketing Campaign Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the **Data Analyst Internship at SkillCraft Technology**.

The objective was to perform **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a bank marketing campaign dataset to understand customer behavior and identify factors influencing term deposit subscriptions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Clean and explore the dataset
- Analyze customer demographics and campaign performance
- Identify factors affecting deposit subscriptions
- Generate business insights and recommendations

---

## 🗂 Dataset Information
- Total Records: **11,162**
- Total Features: **17**
- Target Variable: **Deposit (Yes/No)**

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning
- ✅ Checked for missing values
- ✅ Verified duplicate records
- ✅ Validated data types
- ✅ Prepared dataset for analysis

---

## 📈 Exploratory Data Analysis

The following analyses were performed:

- Deposit Subscription Distribution
- Age Distribution Analysis
- Job-wise Subscription Analysis
- Education-wise Subscription Analysis
- Account Balance vs Deposit Subscription
- Contact Method Effectiveness

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- The dataset contained no missing values or duplicate records.
- Customers with higher account balances were more likely to subscribe to term deposits.
- Certain job categories showed higher subscription rates.
- Customers aged 30–50 years appeared more responsive to marketing campaigns.
- Contact method significantly influenced campaign success.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Focus marketing efforts on high-converting customer segments.
- Prioritize communication channels with better conversion rates.
- Target customers with stronger financial profiles.
- Design personalized campaigns for responsive age groups.
